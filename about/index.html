The whole truth

Posted at — Apr 25, 2022 DRAFT

To tell the truth, the whole truth, and nothing but the truth is a well-known phrase used in court and TV series to affirm that a person’s statements are true and complete.

While this blog is more about science and technology than about court cases, the quest for the whole truth is the often the focus of my posts. In the quest for the “whole truth”, I try to dig a bit deeper than what seems obvious at the first glance.
